About Us

Mark's Pizaa House is the first pizzeria established in Angeles City. Mark's opened for business on 7 July 1961 in Sto Rosario St., Angeles City, where the business has remained since.

Mark's was originally owned by Salvatore Della Bruna, who operated the business in partnership with Franco Fera and in 1968 with Silvio Tuli and Salvatore Mercogliano.

Successful, and well respected, business man Mario Bandera also played a large part. In 1983 the business was purchased by the present owner and Managing Director, Sami Mazloum. Silvana Mercogliano, the wife of former owner Salvatore Mercogliano, credited Mark's with changing the image of Sto Rosario Street from an area with primary appeal to Locals.

In 2007 Mark's Pizza House was inducted as the second member of the World Pizza Hall of Fame, after Lombardi's Pizza in New York City, New York.
